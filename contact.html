Contact Us

Keyverse Games is produced by Mountain Rock State LLC. We're glad to hear from players.

The best way to reach us is by email. We read every message and reply as soon as we reasonably can, but please note we are a small team and cannot guarantee a specific response time.

General email

info@keyversegames.com

What to Contact Us About

  • Support: Trouble loading or playing a game, a level that isn't behaving as expected, or a shortcut you think is listed incorrectly.
  • Accessibility: Feedback on how the games work with assistive technology, keyboard-only use, or on-screen controls, and suggestions for making Keyverse easier to use.
  • Privacy: Questions about our Privacy Policy or how gameplay data is stored in your browser.
  • Business: Partnership, licensing, or other business enquiries regarding Keyverse or Mountain Rock State LLC.

Response Expectations

We aim to respond to support and accessibility messages promptly, though response times may vary depending on volume. For faster help with a gameplay problem, it's useful to include the game name, your browser and device, and a short description of what happened.

    Productivity Boosters

    Volume 1, Game 3 · Keyverse — Mac Shortcut Trainer

    Productivity Boosters focuses on the built-in Mac apps most people use every day. You will practise moving through Safari tabs and the address bar, composing and sorting Mail, building checklists and links in Notes, defining words and previewing files with Spotlight and Quick Look, and handling your session with hide-files and log-out shortcuts. These are the small moves that add up to a faster day.

    What this game teaches

    • Level 1: Safari & Browser — Tabs, Address Bar, Reload
    • Level 2: Mail — Compose, Send, Reply, Forward
    • Level 3: Notes & Reminders — New Note, Checklist, Links
    • Level 4: Spotlight & Quick Look — Define, Eject, Get Info
    • Level 5: Multi-tasking & Session — Lock, Sleep, Hide Files

    How to play

    1. Pick a level from the menu to see the shortcuts it covers.
    2. Read the study list, then press Start Practice.
    3. When prompted, perform each shortcut: press the real keys on your keyboard, or tap the on-screen modifier and action keys, then press Return.
    4. Repeat mode shows the answer first as training wheels; Test Mode hides it so you can check your recall.
    5. Complete every shortcut in a level, then choose Next Level to continue to the next set.

    The game is free, runs entirely in your browser with no download or account, works on desktop, laptop, tablet, and phone, and is available in 12 languages. This reference below stays in English.

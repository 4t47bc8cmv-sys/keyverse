    Daily Driver

    Volume 1, Game 2 · Keyverse — Mac Shortcut Trainer

    Daily Driver picks up where the basics leave off. These are the power-user shortcuts you use dozens of times a day without thinking — flipping to full screen, opening Mission Control, formatting text, selecting by word and line, switching Finder views, and reaching for the right screenshot tool. The goal is speed: keeping your hands on the keyboard instead of the trackpad.

    What this game teaches

    • Level 1: Window Mastery — Mission Control, Spaces, Full Screen
    • Level 2: Text Power Edits — Bold, Italic, Underline, Word Delete
    • Level 3: Selection Speed — Word, Line, and Document Selection
    • Level 4: Finder Pro — Views and Move-Paste Power Moves
    • Level 5: Screenshot & Capture — All Five Native Capture Tools

    How to play

    1. Pick a level from the menu to see the shortcuts it covers.
    2. Read the study list, then press Start Practice.
    3. When prompted, perform each shortcut: press the real keys on your keyboard, or tap the on-screen modifier and action keys, then press Return.
    4. Repeat mode shows the answer first as training wheels; Test Mode hides it so you can check your recall.
    5. Complete every shortcut in a level, then choose Next Level to continue to the next set.

    The game is free, runs entirely in your browser with no download or account, works on desktop, laptop, tablet, and phone, and is available in 12 languages. This reference below stays in English.

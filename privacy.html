Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Keyverse Games is produced by Mountain Rock State LLC ("Mountain Rock State," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information Keyverse Games (the "Service") collects, how it's used, and your choices. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

Keyverse Games is designed to run entirely in your browser with no account or sign-up required. We collect very limited information:

  • Local gameplay data: Your progress, level completion, and language preference may be stored locally in your browser (for example, via localStorage) so the game remembers where you left off. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Standard web logs: Our hosting provider may automatically log basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, consistent with standard web server logging practices.
  • Advertising data (future): If and when we enable advertising through Google AdSense, Google may collect data via cookies to serve relevant ads. See "Advertising" below.

Advertising

Keyverse Games may display ads served by Google AdSense in the future. Google, as a third-party vendor, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. For more information on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy & Terms site.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve the Service — for example, understanding which games are popular or diagnosing technical issues. We do not sell your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Keyverse Games does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Service does not require account creation, and gameplay data is stored locally on your device.

Data Retention & Your Choices

Since gameplay progress is stored locally in your browser, you can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data or localStorage for this site. We do not maintain server-side accounts to delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@keyversegames.com.
